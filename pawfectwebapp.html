Everything you need

Built for how you actually work

Every feature designed by talking to real dog walkers and home organisers — not by guessing.

📅

Smart scheduling

Book dog walks and home organisation sessions in seconds. Set recurring appointments, durations, prices, and notes.

🗺️

Live walk tracking

Start a session and watch the timer run. Track distance, duration, and add notes on the go — all logged per appointment.

📷

Photo uploads

Share walk snapshots or before/after home transformation photos. Clients love seeing their pet or home at its best.

💳

Invoicing & payments

Create invoices in one click. Track paid, pending, and overdue. Accept payments via Stripe with automatic receipts.

🔔

Smart reminders

Never miss a follow-up. Set reminders for appointments, overdue payments, or calls — linked to clients for context.

👥

Client management

Everything about each client in one place: contact details, pet info, service history, notes, and balance at a glance.

📝

Colour-coded notes

Sticky notes per client — access codes, pet quirks, instructions, anything you need to remember on the job.

🌐

Client portal

Give each client their own login to view bookings, photos, and pay invoices online. A premium experience.

📊

Earnings dashboard

See your revenue at a glance. Monthly totals, pending payments, and appointment history — perfect for tax time.
